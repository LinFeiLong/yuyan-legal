Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hear the pronunciation again?

Simply tap the speaker icon to play the audio. You can also tap the turtle icon to hear a slower version of the pronunciation.

Can I learn more than one word per day?

Yuyan is designed around the concept of daily learning. We believe consistent daily practice with one word leads to better retention than cramming multiple words.

What if I miss a day?

No worries! Your current word will remain available until the next day. Learning is a journey, not a race.

Why can't I see previous words?

Yuyan focuses on the present moment of learning. Each day brings a new word to master. This approach helps maintain focus and prevents overwhelm.

Is the app available offline?

Once audio files are downloaded, you can practice pronunciation offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to receive new daily words.

Technical Support

Common Issues

  • Audio not playing: Check your device volume and ensure silent mode is off
  • App crashes: Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, please contact us
  • Login issues: Ensure you're using the correct email address and check your internet connection

Device Requirements

  • iOS 13.0 or later
  • iPhone compatible (iPad supported in compatibility mode)
  • Internet connection for daily updates

About Yuyan

Yuyan (ភាសា) means "language" in Khmer. Our mission is to make learning Khmer accessible, enjoyable, and effective for everyone.

By focusing on daily practice with authentic pronunciation, we help learners build a strong foundation in the Khmer language.
